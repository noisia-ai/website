### Slide 3 — What the conversation shows in `[INDUSTRY]`
Three things you can read directly from public conversation:

1. **The stated objection hides the real one.** "Too expensive" almost always means "I don't trust it'll work."
2. **Share moves in language before it moves in numbers.** How people talk about switching is an early warning your dashboard won't catch.
3. **Cultural permission is narrower than the brief assumes.** Some territories read as aspirational from the outside and as "trying too hard" up close.
